Welcome to FireLine

What is FireLine?

At FireLine, our mission is to harness the power of data and machine learning to enhance awareness and understanding of fires in North America.


We believe that by providing access to 1.88 million meticulously collected data logs and offering cutting-edge image analysis, we can make a significant impact on fire prevention and analysis.


Our commitment is twofold with data driven visualization and machine learning for community driven fire detection and Prediction
